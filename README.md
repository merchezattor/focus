# Focus

Focus is a streamlined task management application designed to bridge the gap between human productivity and AI assistance. It provides a clean, minimalistic interface for organizing your daily work, projects, and goals.

## Purpose

The main goal of Focus is to offer a reliable and fast task manager that doesn't just work for you, but works *with* your AI tools. It aims to simplify how you track your to-dos while serving as a fully accessible workspace for autonomous agents.

## Key Features

- **Core Entities:** A simple hierarchy to organize your life: **Goals** (high-level objectives), **Projects** (structured efforts), and **Tasks** (actionable items).
- **Planning & Mind Map Modes:** Visualize your goals and projects in a flexible, node-based **Mind Map**, and use **Planning Mode** to intelligently orchestrate and schedule your work.
- **Agentic Capabilities (MCP):** Focus is built from the ground up to be fully controllable by AI. Using the Model Context Protocol (MCP), AI agents can seamlessly connect to the project to read, create, update, and manage tasks on your behalf.
- **Human-Agent Collaboration:** Focus keeps everything synchronized. You can check off items manually, or have an AI assistant orchestrate your workflow in the background—all within the same environment.
